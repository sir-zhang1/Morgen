Confronted with massive, heterogeneous, and noisy industrial datasets, I constructed an end-to-end quantitative regression matrix to strip away market redundancy. Utilizing Isolation Forest and DBSCAN algorithms for automated anomaly circuit-breaking, I built a multi-dimensional fusion network based on a Stacking architecture. Through precise calibration of Lasso regression, I achieved an engineering-grade accuracy of $R^2 = 0.962$ within highly uncertain, non-linear causalities. This established a paradigm of locking down physical-world causality through pure mathematical deduction.
